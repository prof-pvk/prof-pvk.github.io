Electromagnetic Theory and Transmission Lines (EMTL)

Professor: P V Krishna Rao

Semester: Spring 2024

Course Description

This course covers the fundamental concepts of electromagnetic fields and their applications in the analysis of transmission lines. It provides insights into electric and magnetic field theories, Maxwell's equations, and wave propagation.
